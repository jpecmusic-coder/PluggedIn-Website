What is PluggedIn

A COMMUNITY
BUILT FOR THE craft.

PluggedIn is where Aotearoa's serious music community gets connected — not through an algorithm, but through real shared experiences. The songwriters, producers, engineers, and artists who want to go deeper, work harder, and build something lasting.

Right now, that means curated Creative Camps starting on the Kāpiti Coast. But the spark is the community — the Creative Camps, seminars, events, and resources are just how we keep it charged.

The Creative Camp Experience

A WEEKEND THAT charges EVERYTHING.

Three days fully disconnected from the everyday — and deeply connected to your craft and the people around you.

Genuine connections

A small, hand-picked group of the most talented creatives in Aotearoa. Not a networking event — a room where real creative friendships and collaborations are sparked.

Uninterrupted creative time

A phone-free morning, no schedule pulling you away, no commute, no life admin. Just the space and time to go deep on your craft — the kind of focused creative block most artists never get.

Content captured for you

A photographer and videographer on-site capturing the weekend — the process, the people, the Sunday playback. Professional content you can actually use, ready to take away.

Real recorded ideas

A dedicated recording station on-site all weekend — interface, mics, laptop, and DAW. Leave with demos, voice memos and captured ideas that are actually production-ready, not just phone recordings.

All meals included

Communal meals from arrival to departure — all fully funded and included. The table is where some of the best ideas happen. No logistics, no splitting bills, just shared food and conversation.

The Sunday playback moment

Every camp ends the same way — everyone shares their best idea from the weekend, filmed. It's the moment the work becomes real. Artists consistently call it the most creatively alive they've felt in years.

Who It's For

BUILT FOR serious CREATIVES.

PluggedIn is curated — every member is chosen deliberately. Here's who the community is built for.

Professional Songwriters

Writing with or for artists, building a catalogue. You take the craft seriously and want a room that meets you at that level.

Producers & Beatmakers

You work with artists or want to. Comfortable collaborating. You know what a good session feels like.

Recording & Mixing Engineers

You want to be creative, not just technical. You know what makes a great capture.

Artists Who Write Their Own Music

Creating original work and wanting to push it further in a focused environment with the right people.

Aotearoa Music Industry Professionals

Embedded in the scene and wanting to connect and collaborate with serious creatives outside the usual context.

The Setting

BEAUTIFUL ENOUGH TO hold the work.

PluggedIn events are held in hand-picked venues around Aotearoa. We choose places that support focused creative work — not just convenient ones.

01Around Aotearoa

We organise events across the motu — starting on the Kāpiti Coast for our first Creative Camps, and expanding to other regions as the community grows. The location is always chosen for its ability to inspire and hold creative work.

02Curated Venues

Every venue is chosen deliberately — enough space for people to spread out, communal areas for shared meals, and an environment that feels genuinely special without being a distraction from the work.

03Starting on Kāpiti Coast

Our first Creative Camps are based on the Kāpiti Coast — native bush, open coastline, an hour from Pōneke. Beautiful enough that you put your phone down without being asked. More locations to follow.
